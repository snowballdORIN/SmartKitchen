PB&J Pancakes



Ingredients


250g peanut butter, smooth or crunchy

5 eggs

4 tbsp natural yogurt

3 tbsp Rasberry Chia Seed Jam, plus extra for serving

1 tbsp rapeseed oil, for frying

4 bananas

Maple Syrup, to serve (optional)




Cooking Instructions


1. Mix the peanut butter and eggs until well combined. The mixture should be a thick dropping consistency.


2. Stir the yogurt and raspberry jam together in a separate bowl thoroughly.


3. Combine the peanut butter and eggs with the yogurt and jam mixtures.


4. Place a large frying pan over a medium heat.


5. Add the rapeseed oil and heat


6. Swirl until the pan is coated with the oil.


7. Add a heaped tablespoon of the mixture to the pan


8. Spread the pancake mixture until it is the thickness of a Scotch pancake.


9. Cook for 2â€“3 minutes, until lightly coloured


10. Turn over and repeat on the other side.


11. Keep the pancakes warm in a low oven or wrapped in a tea towel while you cook the rest of the mixture.


12. Serve with the sliced banana and extra dollops of jam, as well as a drizzle of maple syrup, if desired.
